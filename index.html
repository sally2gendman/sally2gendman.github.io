Dropbox Redesign

I participated in a 24hr design challenge that asked to redesign Dropbox's dashboard to help increase visibility of the types of files users have.

The current version of Dropbox lumps all content together unless you create your own folders. I wanted to create a dashboard that gave the user the ability to do a quick scan of their content as well as the ability to have preset folders.
